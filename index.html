ABOUT US

About The Ramalingeshwara Temple!

Nestled in the serene village of Ramalingapuram, Tamil Nadu, the Ramalingapuram Shiva Temple is a 200-year-old sanctuary of devotion and architectural splendor. This ancient temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is renowned for its rich history. Visitors and devotees are welcomed to experience its divine ambiance and partake in its vibrant cultural traditions. Join us in preserving and celebrating this timeless spiritual heritage.
